About Garcia Builder

Evidence-based online coaching to build strong, athletic bodies that last — with simple nutrition and relentless accountability.

Mission & Vision

Garcia Builder is a coaching company focused on durable, real‑life results. Our mission is to help busy people build muscle, drop fat and feel confident — without extremes. We cut the noise, deliver clear weekly plans, and keep you accountable so progress becomes inevitable.

Andre Garcia — My Journey

I’m Andre Garcia — coach, bodybuilder and builder of systems that make change stick. My journey didn’t start in perfect gyms; it started with discipline forged under pressure and the need to rebuild my life from the ground up. Over the last decade I’ve coached clients in different countries while managing busy gyms, fixing problems others ignored and learning what really sustains progress: clarity, consistency and compassion. I believe in precise programming, simple nutrition and weekly feedback that keeps you honest. No magic, no ego — just work you can repeat. Today I coach online in English, Portuguese and Spanish using Trainerize + WhatsApp. My goal is to help you become the strongest version of yourself — physically and mentally — with results that survive real life.
