About Garcia Builder

We coach real people to build strong, athletic bodies — with simple nutrition, precise training and accountability that sticks.

Mission & Vision

Garcia Builder exists to turn discipline into results. Our mission is to coach busy people to build muscle, drop fat and move with confidence — without crash diets or gimmicks. Guided by the GB mark and its gold standard, we keep the process simple: clear training blocks, flexible nutrition and weekly check‑ins that drive consistency. The vision is a community known for strong bodies and stronger habits, where progress survives real life.

Andre Garcia — My Journey

I began lifting to rebuild more than my body. Over the last twelve years I’ve coached well over a hundred people across different countries and cultures, managed demanding gym floors, and learned to solve problems hands‑on — from fixing equipment that mattered to a 75‑year‑old client, to organizing teams and standards. My background forged a work ethic built on clarity and service. I coach with evidence, not ego: progressive training you can repeat, simple nutrition you can follow, and feedback that keeps you honest. I’m at my best when I’m building systems that make change stick — programs that meet you where you are and move you forward every week. Highlights I’m proud of: guiding 100+ transformations, mentoring newer trainers, and helping members return from pain to performance. The future of Garcia Builder is global and personal at the same time: deeper education content, smarter habit tracking, and coaching in EN/PT/ES that scales without losing the human touch. If you want a stronger body and a clearer process, this is where we start.
