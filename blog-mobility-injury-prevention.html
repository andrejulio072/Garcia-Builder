Mobility & Injury Prevention — Stay Pain‑Free

By Andre Julio Garcia | June 2026

Identify movement limits

Assess range of motion for squat, hinge, lunge, and overhead patterns. Daily mobility drills correct tight spots before they become injuries.

Daily routine (10 minutes)

  • Thoracic rotations
  • Hip flexor and glute activation
  • Scapular stability work

Programming tips

Balance load and volume, avoid repetitive high‑velocity movements without prep, and deload when niggles arise.
