This page explains storage categories used on Garcia Builder Fitness and how to manage your preferences.

Storage Categories

  • Essential / security storage: required for core site security and safe operation.
  • Functionality storage: remembers certain experience preferences.
  • Analytics storage: helps measure site and funnel performance.
  • Advertising storage: supports campaign attribution and ad measurement.
  • Ad user data: controls how ad-related user data may be used.
  • Ad personalization: controls personalized advertising behavior.

How To Reject Or Change

You can reject non-essential categories and reopen cookie settings at any time.

Advertising Identifiers

Where available and permitted by consent, campaign identifiers such as gclid, gbraid, wbraid and fbclid may be captured for attribution.
