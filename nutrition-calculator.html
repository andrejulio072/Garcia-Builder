Practical playbook

Turn your result into a week you can actually follow.

Fat loss

Keep protein high, build meals around lean protein and vegetables, measure calorie-dense fats, and review hunger before cutting harder.

Muscle gain

Use a small surplus, add easy carbs around training, include calorie-dense foods, and watch weekly rate of gain instead of chasing scale jumps.

Recomposition

Prioritise resistance training, stable protein, enough carbs for sessions, and progress photos/measurements instead of scale weight alone.

Busy schedule

Repeat two reliable breakfasts, two lunches and two dinners. Consistency beats variety when work and stress are high.
