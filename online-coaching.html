Questions

Common questions before applying

Who is Garcia Builder Fitness coaching for?

Busy adults who want structure, fat loss support, strength, accountability and a realistic plan around work, family and normal life.

Do I need a gym?

No. Plans can be built for home, gym or a mixed setup, depending on your equipment and routine.

Is this only for fat loss?

No. Fat loss is a common goal, but the coaching also supports strength, muscle gain, routine building and confidence.

How does online coaching work?

You apply, your goal and routine are reviewed, then your training, nutrition targets and weekly check-ins are managed through the coaching app.

What app do clients use?

Clients use the Garcia Builder Coaching App powered by My PT Hub for training, progress tracking and support.

Do I get nutrition support?

Yes. Nutrition support is practical and structured without extreme dieting or fake restrictions.

How do check-ins work?

You submit updates each week so the plan can be adjusted around progress, adherence, schedule and real life.

Can beginners apply?

Yes. Beginners can apply if they are ready for structure, accountability and consistent coaching.
