Your First Workout

A simple, effective Day 1 full-body session to start today. No app required.

Estimated time: 35–45 minutes • Equipment: bodyweight or light dumbbells
1Warm-up (5–7 min)
  • 2 min brisk walk in place or jump rope
  • 10 Arm circles forward + 10 back
  • 10 Hip hinges + 10 Bodyweight squats
  • 30s Plank
2Main Circuit — 3 rounds
  1. Squat or Goblet Squat — 12 reps
  2. Push-ups (knees ok) — 8–12 reps
  3. Hip bridge — 12–15 reps
  4. 1-arm row (dumbbell/backpack) — 10 reps/side
  5. Dead bug — 8 reps/side
  6. Rest — 60–90s
3Finisher (optional)

5 rounds: 20s fast march/jog + 40s easy pace

4Cool down (3–5 min)

Slow breathing + light stretching for hips, chest and upper back.

Form Cues
  • Move with control; leave 2 reps “in the tank”.
  • Spine neutral, heels grounded on squats.
  • Exhale on effort. Keep shoulders away from ears.
