Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-20

This Privacy Policy explains how Garcia Builder Fitness collects, uses, stores and protects personal information submitted through this website and coaching funnels.

Information We Collect

  • Contact data such as first name, email and optional phone/WhatsApp details.
  • Assessment and coaching context data such as fitness goals, barriers, training routine preferences and timeline.
  • Consent records including required delivery consent and optional marketing consent choices.
  • Payment and checkout metadata processed by Stripe for paid services.
  • Attribution and analytics identifiers including campaign UTMs and click IDs where available.

How We Use Information

  • To deliver requested assessment results, resources and customer support responses.
  • To operate coaching services and manage subscriptions.
  • To measure funnel performance and improve website and campaign quality.
  • To send optional marketing communications only where consent was provided.

Processors And Tools

We do not sell personal data. We may process data through service providers needed to run the business, including Supabase (database), Stripe (payments), Brevo/SMTP providers (transactional email), Zapier (automation), and analytics/advertising platforms such as Google Analytics 4, Google Ads and Meta Pixel.

Retention And Security

Data is retained according to operational needs, legal obligations and legitimate business requirements. Reasonable security controls are implemented, but no internet-based system is completely risk-free.

Your Choices And Rights

You may request access, correction or deletion of personal data, and may withdraw optional marketing consent at any time.

International Processing

Depending on provider infrastructure, data may be processed in different regions with safeguards applied by those providers.
