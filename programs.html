How to choose

The right program is the one you can recover from and repeat.

New or returning: start with 3 full-body days, simple nutrition targets and steady weekly progression.
Intermediate: use upper/lower or focused hypertrophy blocks with clear performance targets.
Advanced: rotate specific phases for strength, muscle, conditioning or cutting to avoid spinning wheels.
